Jollof Rice

My favorite Jollof rice recipe

Jollof Rice is a beloved West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, and spices. It's flavorful, vibrant, and perfect for gatherings or everyday meals.

Recipe Information

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty Level: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long grain parboiled rice
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups tomato stew (blended tomatoes, peppers, onions)
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups chicken stock or water

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot and sauté the chopped onions until translucent.
  2. Add the tomato stew and cook for about 10 minutes to reduce acidity.
  3. Stir in thyme, curry powder, seasoning cubes, and salt. Mix well.
  4. Add the rice and stir until it’s fully coated with the sauce.
  5. Pour in chicken stock, cover the pot, and cook on low heat until rice is tender.

Tips

Tip: For extra flavor, add bay leaves or smoked fish. Serve with fried plantains or grilled chicken for a complete meal.
